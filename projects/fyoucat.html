FYOUCAT

Rebellious cat with attitude - Self explanatory

Kitty Rude Iconic

About This Project

FYOUCAT is the ultimate expression of feline sass and rebellion. This iconic middle-finger cat perfectly captures that "I don't care what you think" energy that cat owners know all too well. It's bold, it's unapologetic, and it's relatable AF.

Created using Adobe Photoshop, this piece combines realistic cat photography with bold graphic design. The yellow middle finger contrasts perfectly with the monochrome cat, making a statement that's impossible to ignore. Perfect for stickers, t-shirts, and anyone who appreciates cats with attitude.

Design Process

  • Concept: Captured the perfect sassy cat expression as the foundation
  • Bold Statement: Added the iconic yellow middle finger gesture for impact
  • Color Choice: Used high-contrast yellow to make the message unmissable
  • Final Polish: Refined edges and added subtle glow for that perfect finish
